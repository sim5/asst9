Catch the Runaway

  • High Concept: A policeman trying to catch the runaway prisoner.
  • Player Experience Goals: Tension to avoid the spikes on the floor and the rocks from the bat and to not fall down and get bites from the mice.
  • Genre: Platformer.
  • Game play: The player moves and jumps with arrow keys. The player shoots with 'A' button. The player should not fall down from the platforms nor touch the spikes. Also, the player should avoid the mice and the bat.
  • Prototype goal: The game mechanices are camera movements, jumping and moving and shooting.
  • Features: Scary theme song with the fear of spikes and mice and bat.
  • Setting/Premise: The setting is a jail with a lot of mice, bat and traps everywhere. And there is a runaway prisoner and a policeman is trying to catch him before the prisoner escapes from the jail.
  • Story: A prisoner ran away and a policeman is trying to catch him.
  • Target Audience: Any age with capability to control.
  • Play time: Less than 1 minutes.
  • Strategies: The player should be careful when making jumps and watch out for the wild mice and bat.
